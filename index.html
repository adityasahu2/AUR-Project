About

Aur The Band is a Pakistani band formed in 2020 by Ahad Khan, Usama Ali, and Raffey Anwar who have managed to become a quick favourite among Pakistani music fans. Singers, composers, and producers of the viral fan-favorite song “Tu Hai Kahan”, the song that has crossed 95 million views on YouTube, trended on instagram reels, videos and secured a top spot on many listener’s playlists in Pakistan and beyond. The band was previously known as Uraan but is now known as Aur.

Aur The Band’s music is catchy and upbeat, with lyrics that are both relatable and thought-provoking. Their viral songs, Dooriyan, Chehray and Sometimes have garnered them a large number of fans. The band released their debut single “Tu Hai Kahan” in 2021. The song was a hit in Pakistan and helped to launch the band’s career. They’re known for its blend of R&B and Hip Hop.
